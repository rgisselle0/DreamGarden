Common Daisy - Bellis perennis

This plant can attain maturity in as little as two years, but it can take up to five years to reach that point. English daisies grow slowly at first, but as they mature, they can become weedy. Fun Fact: The word bellis, which derived from Latin, means "pretty." There aren't any significant issues with insects, diseases, or other plants.

Tulips

The most widely recognized connotation of tulips is intense, pure love. Tulips are a traditional flower that have been associated with love for generations since they are so beloved by so many people. They're perfect to give to your partner, kids, parents, siblings, or anybody else you have an intense, unconditional affection for.

Swiss Cheese plant

The name "Swiss Cheese Plant" comes from its large, hole-filled leaves, which resemble Swiss cheese. As the leaves ripen, their shape varies from whole to with holes to eventually with perforations that frequently reach and break through the outer borders of the leaves, giving rise to a pinnatifid leaf shape.
